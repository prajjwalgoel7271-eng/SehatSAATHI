About SehatSaathi

Deploying Edge AI diagnostics to address disparities in remote communities.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower patients and medical volunteers in underserved rural clinics with immediate, client-side, zero-cost disease screening. By using basic web browsers, we remove technological boundaries to early healthcare access.

👁️

Our Vision

To convert any entry-level smartphone or computer into a clinical pre-screening diagnostic console. We envision a future where geographical distance and financial constraints no longer determine a patient's survival rate.

🇮🇳 Healthcare Challenges in Rural India

Primary medical infrastructure in remote Indian districts remains critically overburdened, leaving millions of individuals without prompt diagnostic access.

🏥

Critical Physician Shortage

Rural communities face a severe shortage of clinicians. Waiting periods are long, and patients must travel great distances for standard screening checks.

🪙

Wage & Travel Burdens

Commuting to urban diagnostics centers requires days of travel. High transportation fees combined with lost daily income deter screening.

⌛

Late-Stage Presentation

Over 60% of rural patients seek medical evaluations only when their diseases have reached irreversible, critical stages. Prompt screening saves lives.

🤖

💡 The Importance of AI in Screening

Artificial intelligence changes the dynamics of public screening by making complex assessments completely automated. Instead of sending media feeds (audio cough files or camera finger tapping feeds) to distant servers, SehatSaathi runs algorithms locally on the browser.

  • Complete Privacy: Zero patient records, audio signals, or video parameters leave the local device, complying with strict patient privacy guidelines.
  • Edge Computational Efficiency: Instant results with no bandwidth bottlenecks or expensive hosting server infrastructure requirements.
  • Immediate Guidance: Translates complicated math scores into actionable advice to encourage clinical follow-up.
