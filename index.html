Neurological

🧠 Parkinson's Screening

Assess neurological coordination and stability indicators. This module aggregates four client-side diagnostic metrics to calculate a neurological index score:

  • Finger Tapping: Camera-based movement dexterity checking for speed and amplitude decrement.
  • Vocal Stability: Micro-autocorrelation tracking of voice pitch periods (Jitter, Shimmer, HNR).
  • Spiral Drawing: Math-based analysis of radius deviation using a K-Nearest Neighbors classifier.
  • Reaction Test: Target spotting latency checking pointer coordinate jerk variances.

Blood Health

🩸 Anemia Pallor Scanner

Estimate blood pallor index non-invasively through camera stream evaluation. The scanner runs complete computer vision steps entirely inside the browser:

  • MediaPipe Alignment: Detects face mesh and hand coordinates to pinpoint target screening spots.
  • White-Balance Calibration: Normalizes conjunctiva redness index relative to sclera (eye white) references.
  • Multi-ROI Analysis: Collects red-ratio indicators from your palm, fingernails, and lower eyelids.
Pulmonary

🫁 TB Cough Analyzer

Assess respiratory cough signatures using audio diagnostics. This module utilizes the Web Audio API to capture and analyze acoustic features of cough signals:

  • Acoustic Event Detection: Tracks root-mean-square (RMS) energy to count discrete cough bursts.
  • FFT Frequency Analysis: Runs a local Fast Fourier Transform to find dominant cough frequencies.
  • Spectral Centroid: Computes frequency centroids to distinguish clinical cough signatures from regular clearings.
🫁

Acoustic Wave DSP

Analyzes voice recording waveforms using discrete Fourier bins to extract duration and frequency flags.

📊 AI Health Risk Score

Compile all your health factors into one index. This module maps lifestyle habits and completed screenings to build a health indicator score:

  • Lifestyle Integration: Evaluates sleep hygiene, smoking, and physical activity habits.
  • Screening Aggregator: Auto-queries completed local tests to update risk index ratings.
  • Personalized Plans: Delivers custom advice for nutrition, exercise, and clinical follow-ups.

Medical Disclaimer

SehatSathi is a screening tool, not a diagnostic device. It uses client-side computer vision and audio algorithms to estimate wellness risk indicators. It does NOT replace clinical blood counts, sputum analysis, chest X-rays, or clinical exams from licensed medical practitioners.

All results displayed are statistical estimates, not medical facts. Environmental lighting and microphone backgrounds heavily affect measurement precision.

If this screening system flags any risk profile (mild, moderate, or severe), you should arrange a consultation with a qualified medical specialist for complete diagnostic examinations.
